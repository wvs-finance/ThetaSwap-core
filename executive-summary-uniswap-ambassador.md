# Executive Summary: Fiscal Policy Tooling for Uniswap v4 Hooks
## Facilitating Economic Expertise in Pool Design

---

## The Problem

**Current State**: Protocol engineers with economic/finance backgrounds struggle to implement sophisticated pool behaviors because they must learn complex crypto-specific terminology (fee deltas, balance deltas, hook mechanics) instead of using familiar economic concepts.

**Business Impact**: 
- Economic expertise is underutilized in pool design
- Learning curve barrier prevents sophisticated economic models
- Protocol engineers prefer familiar terminology over crypto jargon
- Revenue concentration issues (JIT vs PLP) lack economic solutions

---

## The Solution

**Fiscal Policy Tooling**: Hook-based tooling that enables protocol engineers to design custom tax mechanisms over pool economic agents using familiar economic terminology.

**Key Features**:
- **Economic Terminology Translation**: Maps familiar terms (progressive taxation, Pigouvian taxes) to hook mechanics
- **Custom Tax Mechanisms**: Design sophisticated tax policies over pool economic agents
- **Learning Facilitation**: Helps economists understand crypto concepts through familiar terms
- **Revenue Distribution**: Solves JIT vs PLP concentration through economic principles

---
