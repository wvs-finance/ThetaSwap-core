\begin{abstract}
We derive a complete CFMM specification for a perpetual fee concentration
derivative. The LP payoff $\VLP(p) = \ln(1+p)$ replicates protection against
adverse competition in liquidity provision, where $p = A_T/(1-A_T)$ is the
odds-ratio price of the fee concentration index $\AT$.

The derivation follows the oracle-assisted replication framework of
Angeris et al.\ (2021) and the geometric relationships of
Angeris et al.\ (2023). The resulting CFMM has trading function
$\psi(x,y) = y + \ln(x) + 1 - x = 0$, with replication-preserving fees
and discrete funding at liquidity events.

We establish 31 invariants across 7 tiers: structural, economic, rate,
state-machine, replication, perpetual, and fee-concentration-specific.
\end{abstract}
