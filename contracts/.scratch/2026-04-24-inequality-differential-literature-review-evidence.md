## Sparse-axis flags (detail)

### `LIT_SPARSE_onchain_inequality`
arxiv categories `cs.CR`, `q-fin.GN`, `econ.GN` return zero hits for `(stablecoin OR DeFi OR on-chain) AND (inequality OR labor-capital differential OR progressive hedge)`. Closest hits are (i) Jones-Matsui-Knottenbelt (stablecoin as indicator, but for crypto not macro-inequality); (ii) Firouzi (tokenized sovereign debt, not inequality); (iii) Yamada 2026 UBI time-decaying currency (arxiv:2602.18714) — adjacent welfare-redistribution instrument but not a differential hedge.

### `LIT_SPARSE_Colombia_arxiv`
arxiv searches for `Colombia AND (exchange rate OR consumption OR carry trade OR inequality)` return primarily (i) Gasparini-Reyes Latam inequality paper (not Colombia-specific); (ii) Tello-Carvache dollarization Latam (covers Ecuador primarily); (iii) unrelated engineering/physics Colombian author papers. Colombia-specific macroeconometrics lives in BanRep Borradores de Economía grey literature — requires off-arxiv fetch in Task 11.O preparation.

**Recommended Colombia-specific Borradores fetch targets** (URLs verified live at BanRep):
- Borradores series on "tasa de cambio y pass-through" (TRM-pass-through papers)
- Borradores series on "tasa de política monetaria" (BanRep policy rate dynamics)
- Borradores series on "consumo de los hogares" (household consumption)
- DANE methodological notes on EMCM and ECV (retail-sales + household-consumption survey harmonization)

## Axis-3 Parametric-Instrument Evidence Extensions

Beyond core references in main report:
- Argentina 2005 GDP-linked warrant restructuring — 2005 default exchange; paid based on real GDP growth above a threshold; documented failures of design (strike too-tight).
- Greece 2012 PSI GDP-linked securities — similar structure, illiquid secondary market.
- Academic follow-up: Consiglio-Tumminello-Zenios 2018 (above) prices S-CoCo with regime-switching hidden Markov — methodologically closest to Abrigo inequality-instrument pricing primitive.
- Pang-Choi 2022 Bayesian-NN parametric insurance is the state-of-the-art on data-driven parametric trigger design — cite as methodological precedent for filtering the X signal.

## Axis-4 On-Chain / DeFi Precedent Evidence

Searches probed:
- `"stablecoin" AND economic indicator AND country` → Jones-Matsui-Knottenbelt 2026 (crypto-market, not country-macro).
- `DeFi AND inequality AND hedge` → zero hits.
- `on-chain AND labor share` → zero hits.
- `progressive hedge AND blockchain` → zero hits.

**Conclusion:** the on-chain inequality-differential instrument is genuinely novel. We do not find prior work that anticipates the construct. This is not a "we didn't search hard enough" gap — the axis is intrinsically sparse because the domain is young (post-2022 stablecoin-flow-as-indicator literature; post-2020 DeFi derivatives literature). Opportunity: Abrigo can claim priority if design is published formally.
