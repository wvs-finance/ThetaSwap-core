**Fix required:** (a) name canonical power formulation, (b) embed exact scipy call in the constant docstring, (c) replace "≈ 0.85" with the actual reproduced figure, (d) reconcile cited "0.37–0.42 at MDES=0.20" with my Method-1 0.245.
